Rishikesh


Rishikesh, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the Ganges River, is renowned for its spiritual ambiance and vibrant yoga culture. Often called the "Yoga Capital of the World," it attracts seekers from around the globe with its ashrams, meditation centers, and serene landscapes. The iconic Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula suspension bridges, along with thrilling white-water rafting opportunities, add to its charm. Rishikesh offers a perfect blend of tranquility, adventure, and spirituality.
